MY STORY

How I Found My Freedom

From civil servant
to cruise nomad

Chapter 1

I was a civil servant

Chasing the "right answers"
Sometimes the stable desk
felt more suffocating than safe

"Is this all my life will be?"
The question kept circling
in my mind

Chapter 2

I quit and left

"What a waste," they said
Letting go of stability
took more courage than expected

But I decided to live once
The world was wider than I thought
and it was waiting for me

Chapter 3

A new world on cruises

First day working at sea
In that moment, I knew
"Oh, you can live this way too"

Through 100+ cities
every day became a new beginning
I learned there's no
single right answer

NOW

Now, I'm free

The word "stability"
no longer confines me

I live each day
with excitement, not anxiety

This is the freedom
I found for myself

"Not following someone else,
but living as yourself."
